Company Profile Page

Company details UI to show company details. In addition the following actions can be carried out:

  1. Set bespoke currency for the company.
  2. View company statistics.
  3. Define headers mapping.
  4. Edit company information.
  5. Delete company.
  6. Attach a parent company.
  7. View the hierarchy the company belongs to.
  8. Attach managing account.
An additional "Employees" section was added (out of our scope) just to show the linking possibilities when dealing with accounts.
